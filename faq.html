Q: Whats the best way to store my suraface?

The best way to store your hand-painted surface is to roll it up in the tube in which you received the surface. You may also roll up multiple surfaces together and store them in one tube. Lastly, you may also lay them up straight on a flat surface and cover them. It is a good way to get rid of dust, dirt or creases. Make sure the tubes are placed in the clean and dry environment and away from direct sunlight. The damp environment and sunlight may lead to color change and fade the backdrop.

Your hand-painted surface is a piece of art.

  • DO NOT fold a hand-painted surface!
  • DO NOT wash a hand-painted surface!
  • DO NOT stand or place your hand on a hand-painted surface!
  • DO NOT place knives or sharp-edge objects unsupervised on a hand-painted surface!

Store flat or in the tube so when you extend it will lie flat. With proper care and storage, you can keep your Rainbow Surface looking great from year to year and provide professional photo effect for ages!
